KBIA Profile Image Tool

Show Your Support for KBIA on Facebook and Twitter

This super easy to use tool will add a little KBIA blurb to your profile picture and let you upload a new copy. Here's how it works:

  1. You click "Let's Do It!" below.
  2. Our app will ask for permission to see your Facebook profile.
  3. Once you've said yes, the app will generate a new profile picture and take you to it.
  4. To set this as your photo on Facebook and Twitter, just right (or control) click, say "Save Image As...", save the photo somewhere you'll remember and upload as normal!
